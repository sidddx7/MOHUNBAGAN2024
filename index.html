LEGACY

Mohun Bagan Super Giant, an Indian football club based in Kolkata, West Bengal, has a rich legacy of success and achievements: Founded in 1889 Mohun Bagan is one of the oldest football clubs in Asia

FANATICAL

Mohun Bagan boasts one of the most passionate and dedicated fan bases in India, creating an electrifying atmosphere at their matches. Their unwavering support, expressed through vibrant chants, synchronized tifo displays, and unwavering loyalty, makes the Mariners' home ground a fortress for the opposition

HISTORIC

Mohun Bagan has recently clinched several major trophies, solidifying their position as a dominant force in Indian football. In the 2022-2023 season, they secured the Indian Super League (ISL) Championship title. Building on this success, they further enhanced their trophy cabinet by winning the Durand Cup and the ISL League Winners Shield in the 2023-2024 season.
